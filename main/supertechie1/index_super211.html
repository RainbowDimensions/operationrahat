The Super21 Team1 is working on a Live Project on "Surface Computing".
Surface Computing is touch based graphical user interface that can recognize multiple objects that are placed on the surface and distinguish the difference between them which allows manipulation and direct control of digital content.

1)Direct Interaction: interacting with touch instead of a keyboard or mouse
2)Multitouch: several points of contact at once
3)Multiuser: many users collaborating at one time
4)Object Recognition: physical objects have tags to recognize and trigger different responses
