VISION...

We believe that kids of this generation have a strong inclination towards technology and potential to achieve it. Hence, we want to lay a strong foundation which will lead them to a brighter future by enhancing their creativity and making them independent, tech savvy and confident to step up as an entrepreneur. We further wide our gaze by making them bold and fearless, so that they can accept any challenge that is coming their way.

MISSION...

By year 2020, we want Robology as a subject to be collaborated within Maharashtra Board Syllabus. Moreover we want every student to willingly join hands with the ever-updating technology and make the most of it by contributing towards the trending technological world. By achieving our goal in contributing urban areas we further plan to spread our tentacles/ reach out to rural places by lending hand in undeveloped areas and helping them with everyday challenges.

WHAT IS ROBOLOGY ?

Robology is a platform where we encourage students who loves technology and aims to be a technocrat and hence has taken an audacious step to break the old cliché method of imparting knowledge among students and has put forward different way of teaching where from day 1 students are trained to learn things practically. The entire module consist 70% practical and 30% theory where students go through an awesome experience of learning and comes for more every day. We think about new creative ways to engage minds by giving interactive technical presentations as well as seminars & sharing a fundamental vision with institution, colleges & other that are committed to us for future development. Our association pioneered the global delivery model which believe in the excellence, where sharpening innovative projects & sharing of knowledge among the students in the field of Research & development. Instead of these, It facilitates to aware about the upcoming technology by arranging Regular workshops, competition & motivates them to build up their interest in elected field & also believe that inspiring young Minds in the field of Science & Technology can help nation to place it in higher technical level.

WHY ROBOLOGY ?

The technological world around us is evolving every day and these changes are not led by nature but humans. So to match up with their footsteps, we have to strive hard to attain that level by maximizing our skill and creativity. This can be made possible if, we together collaborate and step on the ladder of success where at every step we hold your hand, build your inner strength, and develop confidence under proper guidance and help. What we learn in school is only limited to theoretical knowledge, but the world doesn’t end there,a lot more still remains to be studied and discussed hence we provide a professional technical education that emphasises in escalating your creativity by giving 360 degree knowledge and practical approach.

HOW ROBOLOGY ?

We provide best project training with a Robotic kit, embedded live projects, Regular Workshops and Live Projects. We believe that children learn more when the atmosphere around them is cordial, fun-loving and interactive; we have carry-forwarded this approach and hence every session held is always joyful, interactive and enthusiastic. We make sure every student actively participates by giving his/her 100% and ardently resolves all their doubts and difficulties.
